2 ounces freshly squeezed orange juice
1 ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 ounce orgeat
1 1/2 ounces white rum
1/2 ounce gin
1/2 ounce brandy
1/2 ounce Amontillado sherry
Directions
1.Add everything except sherry to a cocktail shaker and fill with ice. Shake well and strain into an ice-filled highball glass. Carefully pour the sherry on top of the drink; garnish with a sprig of mint.
